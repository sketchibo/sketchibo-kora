KORA is no longer just a chat system. It is becoming a directing engine for AI-generated media.

Instead of treating writing, images, and video as separate tasks, it can break a transcript into meaningful visual beats.

Each beat can be matched to the right type of shot, the right model, and the right amount of effort depending on the available time budget.

In a quick mode, KORA can produce a rough but coherent visual plan. In a longer mode, it can generate better prompts, stronger hero shots, and more polished continuity.

Eventually, KORA will route work across image APIs, video APIs, and tools like Sora, turning one transcript into a complete production pipeline.
